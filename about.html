ABOUT MARVEL ACADEMY

About the Owner

You'all meet Afolayan Marvellous Oladipo is the visionary founder of Marvel Academy. A graduate of Anchor University, where he studied Information Technology, mr Afolayan hails from Kwara State, Nigeria. With a profound commitment to education and a passion for nurturing young minds, he established this school to provide a high-quality educational foundation for children. As a dedicated family man,mr Afolayan is blessed with three wonderful children: Marvellous Jr, Saphire, and Diamond. His personal experience as a parent drives his dedication to creating a school environment that is both academically enriching and supportive of each child's unique needs.

Our Programmes

At Marvel Academy, our programs are designed to cater to the diverse needs of our students:

  • Early Years Foundation Stage (EYFS): Our EYFS program focuses on play-based learning, ensuring our youngest learners develop essential skills in a nurturing environment.
  • Primary Education: Our primary curriculum covers a broad range of subjects, including Mathematics, Science, Language Arts, Social Studies, and Physical Education, tailored to spark curiosity and a love for learning.
  • Extra-Curricular Activities: We offer a variety of extra-curricular activities such as sports, music, art, and drama to help students explore their interests and talents beyond the classroom.
